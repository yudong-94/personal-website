### ***Trust Level of Information Sources***

AI is becoming a daily tool that improves productivity greatly both in work and outside of work. However, AI tools still hallucinate from time to time. Ho much do people trust them against other information sources? 

#### My Visualization

I made the visualization with a heatmap that is colored based on the percentage of responses.
