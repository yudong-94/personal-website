### ***Cost of Living China vs. India***

I came across a visualization of the cost of living in China vs. India on [Voronoi](https://www.voronoiapp.com/economy/China-vs-India-The-Cost-of-Everyday-Things-7991) today -- it offers some good insights, so I decided to visualize it in my own way.  

#### My Visualization

I chose the Gantt chart again, as it is a great way to show the difference between two categories and across multiple line items.

#### Insights
* As the monthly net salary is higher in China, most things on the list are more expensive in China, with the exception of Cappuccino and beer;
* The item that has the highest cost difference is the monthly fitness club membership -- it costs almost 3x in China ($41.25 vs. $14.75).
