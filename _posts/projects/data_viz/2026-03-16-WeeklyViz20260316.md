### ***Population vs. Households Around the Wrold***

My visualization this week looks at the population vs. # of households across the world. I got this data from [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_countries_by_number_of_households).  

#### My Visualization

This week's visualization is a scatter chart -- x-axis is the population and y-axis is the # of households in each country. Please note both axes are log-scaled due to the high variance in population size. The size of each bubble is the average household size.
