### ***AI Hallucination vs. Accuracy***

AI models are advancing quickly and there are more and more players in this field. However, a common challenge with all AI models is hallucination. Though I like how AI model makes coding easier, I still cannot trust the code it generated 100%. This week, the dataset I am visualizing is the hallucination vs. accuracy of various AI models. The original visualization was posted on [Visual Capitalist](https://www.voronoiapp.com/technology/Which-AI-Models-Hallucinate-the-Most-7211).  

#### My Visualization

This is a scatter plot showing accuracy vs. halluciation. Models from the same company share the same color.
