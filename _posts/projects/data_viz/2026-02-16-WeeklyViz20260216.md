### ***Most Posted Jobs in the US***

This week, the dataset I visualize is about the most posted jobs in the US, published by [Lightcast](https://lightcast.io/resources/blog/most-posted-for-jobs-in-each-us-state). It has the top 3 needed jobs by state.  

#### My Visualization

My visualization is a hexmap with a parameter to switch among the top 1st, 2nd, and 3rd most posted jobs.
