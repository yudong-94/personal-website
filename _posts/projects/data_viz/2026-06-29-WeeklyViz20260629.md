### ***Real Cost of a Degree Abroad***

This week's visualization is based on [Voronoi](https://www.voronoiapp.com/education/Ranked-The-Real-Cost-of-a-Degree-Abroad-8492) and compares the estimated cost recovery time for earning a degree abroad across major study destinations.

#### My Visualization

This visualization ranks countries by how many years of estimated graduate salary it would take to pay back a four-year international degree, making the slowest and fastest recovery paths easy to compare.

#### Insights
* The six slowest-payback destinations are the U.K., Canada, New Zealand, Ireland, the U.S., and Australia;
* Austria and Germany sit at the fast-payback end, with both below 2.5 estimated years.
