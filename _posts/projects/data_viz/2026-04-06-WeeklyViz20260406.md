### ***Most Congested Cities in the US***

The visualization this week is based on the [Global Traffic Score report](https://inrix.com/scorecard/#form-download-the-full-report) by INRIX. They measure the time lost due to traffic congestion and its corresponding monetary value.  

#### My Visualization

This visualization is a table with four columns -- Urban area, global rank of congestion, annual hours lost from congestion, and change from 2024. The last two columns are presented as bar charts.

#### Insights
* There are four US cities among the top 10 most congested cities in the world -- Chicago (3rd), NYC (4th), Philadelphia (5th), and Los Angeles (10th);
* Most of these top congested US cities saw an increase in hours lost compared to 2024. The two with the most significantly worse traffic conditions are Philadelphia (31%) and Baltimore (31%).
