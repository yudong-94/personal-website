#### Insights
* Inflation and cost of living rose from 39% to 48%, moving from third place in 2019/20 to the top concern in 2025/26;
* Housing entered the latest top list at 37%, while immigration fell from 40% to 30% and moved from second place to the bottom of the displayed latest list.
