### ***Coffee Consumption Worldwide***

I have many coworkers and friends drink multiple cups of coffee every day to keep a clear mind during the day time. Though I am more of a tea person, I am curious which countries are more addicted to coffee. Therefore, this week, I found a dataset from [Visual Capitalist](https://www.visualcapitalist.com/ranked-which-country-consumes-the-most-coffee/) and made a visualization on worldwide coffee consumption myself.   

#### My Visualization

This visualization is a radial chart, where each bar represents the daily coffee consumption (cups) and ordered in a circle.      

*Please note that all the visualizations are designed for desktop view, so it is recommended to view them on a desktop device.*

#### Insights
* Europeans are much more addicted to coffee than people around the world -- the top three countries by coffee consumption are Luxembourg, Finland, and Sweden;
* Meanwhile, countries in South Asia, South East Asia and Africa tend to have a much lower coffee consumption level.
