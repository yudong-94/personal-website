#### Insights
* AI adoption varies from 5% to 60% around the world -- the highest is 64% in UAE and the lowest is 5.1% in Cambodia. Meanwhile, the world average is 16.3%, increased by 1.2% since H1.  
* Generally speaking, Europe and North America show higher AI adoption, while Africa has the lowest AI adoption.
