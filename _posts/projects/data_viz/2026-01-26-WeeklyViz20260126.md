### ***Food Delivery Prices***

Since I joined DoorDash, I have been more interested in the food delivery service industry. I also use DoorDash, UberEats and Grubhub as a user myself every month. Therefore, [this visualization](https://www.voronoiapp.com/food-beverage/The-Most-Affordable-Food-Delivery-Service-by-State-7547) on the most affordable food-delivery service by state is very interesting to do and I decided to make my own version of it.
