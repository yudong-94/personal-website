### ***World Coffee Production***

This week's visualization is based on [Our World in Data](https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/coffee-bean-production?country=VNM~COL~BRA) and compares green coffee bean production in Brazil, Vietnam, and Colombia.

#### My Visualization

This visualization tracks how coffee production has changed over time across the three countries, making it easy to compare Brazil's long-running lead with Vietnam's rapid rise.

#### Insights
* While South America remains the largest coffee bean production source, Asian coffee bean production has increased 18x since 1960;
* Vietnam passed Colombia in 1999 and became the second largest producer among the selected countries, behind Brazil.
