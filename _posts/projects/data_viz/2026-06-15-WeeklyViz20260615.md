### ***AI's Job Bias***

This week's visualization is based on [Visual Capitalist](https://www.visualcapitalist.com/cp/male-doctors-female-cashiers-ais-job-bias/) and compares the gender representation of 15 professions in videos generated by four leading AI tools.

#### My Visualization

This visualization compares how frequently women and men appear in AI-generated depictions of each profession, highlighting where gender patterns are consistent across models and where the tools disagree.

#### Insights
* Nine of the 15 professions skew male in every model, including CEO, engineer, politician, doctor, lawyer, and architect;
* Teacher depictions vary by 63 percentage points, from 15.8% women in Hailuo Minimax to 79.0% in Veo 3.
