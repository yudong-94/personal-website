### ***US Government Shutdowns***

We just had the longest government shutdowns in the US -- 43 days from 10/1/2025 to 11/12/2025. This week, I collected all the US govnerment shutdowns in the history from [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_shutdowns_in_the_United_States) and made a visualization.  

#### My Visualization

This visualization is a simple bar chart that shows the US government duration by historical shutdowns, with the color representing the political parties of the US presidents.       

*Please note that all the visualizations are designed for desktop view, so it is recommended to view them on a desktop device.*
