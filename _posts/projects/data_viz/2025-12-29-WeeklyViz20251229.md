### ***Christmas Gift Google Search Trend***

Do you go to Google to get Christmas gift inspirations? This week I extracted the search index of "christmas gift" in the last 90 days on [Google Trend](https://trends.google.com/trends/explore?date=today%203-m&geo=US&q=christmas%20gift&hl=en) to make a fun visualization.   

#### My Visualization

This visualization is a simple line chart with the christmas tree color :)

#### Insights
* "Christmas Gift" search started to spike in end of Nov, probably also related to the Black Friday holiday sales;
* After that, its seach popularity increases significantly every weekend. Surprisingly, the highest search index actually happened on the Chrsitmas eve day itself, probably lots of people still doing last-minute shopping.
