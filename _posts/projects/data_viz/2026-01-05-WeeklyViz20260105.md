### ***US Top 1% Income***

A new year started, which means another tax season is coming. This week, the dataset I visualized is the top 1% income by states in 2025 based on IRS tax return data. The original dataset comes from [smartasset](https://smartasset.com/data-studies/top-1-percent-income-2025).   

#### My Visualization

I used a hex map to show income by states in a more compact way.

#### Insights
* Connecticut is the only state where it takes more than $1 million to be a top 1% earner. This is likely because it concentrates extremely high-paying finance and executive jobs next to New York City, combined with a small population and a very top-heavy income distribution;  
* Generally speaking, states on the west and east coasts have higher income floor to be a top 1% earner.
