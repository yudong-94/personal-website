### ***International Visitors to the US***

This week, I visualized the [I-94](https://www.trade.gov/i-94-arrivals-program?anchor=content-node-t14-field-lp-region-2-1) data of international visitors to the US. For those who don't know, `The National Travel and Tourism Office (NTTO) manages the International Visitor Arrivals Program. This program is sometimes called the Arrival and Departure Information System (ADIS) I-94 because it is the name of the record that is completed when the visitor enters the United States.` It has valuable data of the country of residence of all visitors admitted to the US.  

#### My Visualization

This visualization is a time series line chart organized in a grid for easier comparison both by country/region and by year.

#### Insights
* After the pandemic (2020-2022), we see a steep recovery in visitors in most countries;
* Canada and Mexico account for the majority of international visitors to the US, given how close they are geographically;
* Visitors from Oceania, Middle East, Africa remain small but growing slowly.
