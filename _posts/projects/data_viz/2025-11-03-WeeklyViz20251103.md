### ***Avg Jobs by Ages***

Have you ever wondered how many jobs Americans hold from 18 to 58, and during what time do they change jobs most often? My visualization this week is based on a report from [U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics](https://www.bls.gov/opub/ted/2025/people-born-from-1957-to-1964-held-an-average-of-12-9-jobs-from-ages-18-to-58.htm), looking at jobs held by people born between 1957 and 1964.  

#### My Visualization

This visualization is a bar chart grouped by age groups and gender.

#### Insights
* People born between 1957 and 1964 held an average of 12.9 jobs from ages 18 to 58. This number is slightly higher for men (13.1) vs. women (12.7);
* Ages 18 to 24 is when people change their job most frequently. On average, everyone held 5.6 jobs during these ages;
* With people getting older, they get less likely to change jobs.
