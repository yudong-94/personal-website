### ***Migration to/from California***

People move to and move out of California all the time. But where are the most common sources and where are the most popular destinations? This week, I got the state-to-state migration data from [the US Census Bureau](https://www.census.gov/data/tables/time-series/demo/geographic-mobility/state-to-state-migration.html) and visualized residents' migration to/from California.  

#### My Visualization

This visualization has two [sankey charts](https://exchange.tableau.com/products/932), showing migrations into and out of California respectively.

#### Insights
* California is one of the most popular migration destinations for foreigners -- out of the 738k residents who migrated to CA, 316k (43%) are from abroad;
* As for the people migrating out of California, the most common destinations are Texas (14%), Arizona (8%), and Washington (6%).
