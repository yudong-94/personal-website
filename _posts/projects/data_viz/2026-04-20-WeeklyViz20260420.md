### ***Gasoline Prices Changes***

This week's visualization is based on [Global Petrol Prices](https://www.globalpetrolprices.com/fuel_price_trend_Iran_war.php) and compares how gasoline prices changed across countries after the start of the Iran war.

#### My Visualization

This visualization uses a ranked bar chart so it is easy to compare how sharply gasoline prices moved across countries and to spot the few places where prices were flat or slightly down.

#### Insights
* Since the Iran war, most countries have seen gasoline prices increase, and the top 10 countries all recorded increases of at least 37%;
* There were still five countries where gasoline prices did not increase: Russia, Madagascar, Zambia, Barbados, and Colombia.
