### ***% of Population Living in a House in Europe***

#### Insights
* House living became less common in most of the listed geographies: 21 declined, 9 increased, and 1 was flat between 2010 and 2024;
* Ireland still had the highest share of people living in a house in 2024 at 90.2%, while Malta saw the largest drop (-14.3 percentage points) and Poland posted the biggest increase (+5.4 points).
