Global traffic pain is broadening beyond the headline leaders

Istanbul sits at the top of INRIX's impact ranking, but some of the sharpest deterioration is happening further down the list, especially in Turkish and U.S. metros that are climbing fast.

Darker orange marks places where congestion worsened fastest from 2024 to 2025, while blue marks cities that eased.

Without hover, the main story is straightforward: Istanbul leads the published ranking, Chicago loses slightly more raw time than Mexico City, and some of the fastest worsening sits outside the top 10.
